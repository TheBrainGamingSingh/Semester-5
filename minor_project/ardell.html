Ardell Wellness Stress Test

Rate your satisfaction with each of the following items by using this scale:

+3 = ecstatic, +2 = very happy, +1 = mildly happy, 0 = indifferent, -1 = mildly disappointed, -2 = very disappointed, -3 = completely dismayed

1. Choice of career

2. Present job/business/school/college

3. Marital status

4. Primary relationships

5. Capacity to have fun

6. Amount of fun exprienced in the last month

7. Financial prospects

8. Current income level

9. Spirituality

10. Level of self-esteem

11. Prospects of having an impact on those who know you and possibly others

12. Sex life

13. Body, how it looks and performs

14. Home life

15. Life skills and knowledge of issues and facts unrelated to your job or profession

16. Learned stress management capacities

17. Nutritional knowledge, attitudes and choices

18. Ability to recover from disappointment, hurt, setbacks and tragedies

19. Confidence that you currently are or in the future will be reasonably close to your highest potential

20. Achievement of a rounded or balanced quality of life

21. Sense that life for you is on an upward curve, getting better with time

22. Level of participation in issues and concerns beyond your immediate interests

23. Choice whether to parent or not and with the consequences or results of your choice

24. Role in some kind of network of friends, relatives and others whom you deeply care about

25. Emotional acceptance of the inescapable reality of aging
